Site Purpose

The purpose of this CornChamber Taber Chamber of Commerce sight is to bring important information about Taber, it's businesses and events, all into one place. This site will allow businesses that reside in Taber to network with each other. There will be different membership levels for the businesses to sign up for within this site that will allow them to access different benefits based on what level they sign up for. This will allow businesses to grow within the community and will provide necessary advertising for said businesses. This site will not only be for businesses though. It will also be for the community of Taber, its individuals. There will be events that are being hosted by the Town of Taber as well as special events being hosted by our Chamber of Commerce businesses posted here on this site.
