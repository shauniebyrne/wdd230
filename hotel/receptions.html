Wedding Receptions

Services Offered To Those Wanting To Rent The Reception Hall For Wedding Receptions

Decorations

  • Offer a variety of table place setting decorations and linens
  • Offer a variety of room decorations including flowers
  • Chairs provided with optional chair covers (many different colors to choose from)
  • Tableware provided
  • Larger equipment rentals available

Catering Services

  • Offer a variety of cuisine styles and ethnic flavors to compliment the many tastes of your guests.
  • Appetizers
  • Plated Cuisine or Buffets
  • Desserts
  • Optional (there is kitchen access for food preperation and serving)

Wedding Cake

  • Many different designs (consultation meeting at least 3 weeks before reception)
  • Sizes vary based on guest count and couple's preference
  • Many different flavour combinations
  • Sample taste testing, by appointment, expected and done at consultation
  • Optional (can opt to bring your own cake)

Overall Pricing

  • Reception Hall rental: $1,000 (tables and chairs included)
  • Reception Hall with decorations: Starting at $1,500
  • Reception Hall with decorations and catering: Starting at $2,100
  • Reception Hall with decorations, catering, wedding cake: Starting at $2,300
  • * Pricing starts at base requirements

Individual Pricing

  • Decorations: Start at $500 (will increase with duplicity of decorations and an addition of flowers and equipment rental)
  • Catering - Banquet Meal and Dessert: $600
  • Catering - Banquet Appetizers, Meal, and Dessert: $800
  • Catering - Plated Cuisine and Dessert: $1,000
  • Catering - Plated Appetizers, Meal, and Dessert: $1,200
  • Wedding Cake - Two Tiered: $200
  • Wedding Cake - Three Tiered: $300
